    DNA Sequencing Facility

    Aaron Jeffries, Director Sequencing Facility, Nanopore Day 2022

    The University of Exeter Sequencing Facility provides state-of-the-art genomics and bioinformatics analysis. We specialise in providing a bespoke sequencing services to researchers in academia and industry.

    A good experimental design is crucial to ensure you obtain high quality, publishable data. We’re here to help you make sure your design is optimal and provides the best value for money without compromising your science.
